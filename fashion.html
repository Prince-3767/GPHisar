Fashion Design

Diploma in Fashion Design - 3 Year Program

About the Department

The Fashion Design department builds practical skills in garment making, pattern development, illustration, and textile understanding. Students learn how to turn ideas into finished apparel through hands-on studio work, sewing practice, and creative design exercises that support both industry jobs and self-employment.

Program Duration

3 Years Diploma (6 Semesters) | Affiliated to HSBTE | AICTE Approved

Department Highlights

Creative and practical training for apparel design, sewing, and styling.

Key Subjects / Curriculum

✓ Fashion Illustration and Design Basics
✓ Pattern Making and Garment Construction
✓ Draping and Surface Ornamentation
✓ Textile Science and Fabric Selection
✓ Computer-Aided Fashion Design
✓ Merchandising and Portfolio Development

Important Labs & Facilities

Garment Construction Lab

Sewing machines, finishing tools, and practical garment-making exercises.

Pattern Making Studio

Drafting tables and tools for pattern development and fitting practice.

Fashion Illustration Lab

Drawing, sketching, color theory, and portfolio presentation work.

Textile & CAD Room

Fabric study, design software, and computer-based fashion work.

Career Opportunities

• Fashion designer or assistant designer in apparel units
• Pattern maker, tailor, or garment production assistant
• Merchandising and quality checking support roles
• Boutique owner, self-employed designer, or studio assistant
• Higher studies in fashion, apparel, or design management
