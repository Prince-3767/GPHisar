Our History

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1992

Guru Daksh Government Polytechnic, Hisar was established in 1992 by the Government of Haryana under the Department of Technical Education. Named after the revered saint Guru Daksh, the institute was founded with the vision to provide quality technical education to the youth of Haryana and beyond.

Over three decades, the institute has grown from offering just a few programs to now providing 13+ diploma courses across engineering, technology, and design disciplines. Our alumni serve in leading organizations across India and abroad.

Spread across a lush 40-acre campus in Hisar, the institute boasts modern laboratories, well-equipped workshops, a central library, hostels, sports facilities, and smart classrooms — all designed to foster holistic development of students.

Welcome

Principal's Message

Dear Students, Parents, and Well-Wishers,

It is my great pleasure and privilege to welcome you to Guru Daksh Government Polytechnic, Hisar — one of the premier technical institutions in the state of Haryana. Since our establishment in 1992, we have been steadfastly committed to the cause of quality technical education and holistic development of our students.

Our institution is not merely a center for academic learning; it is a vibrant community where young minds are nurtured, skills are honed, and futures are shaped. With a dedicated faculty of over 150 members, state-of-the-art laboratories, well-equipped workshops, and modern smart classrooms, we provide an environment that is conducive to both learning and innovation.

At Guru Daksh Government Polytechnic, we believe that true education goes beyond textbooks. Our curriculum is designed to balance theoretical knowledge with practical skills, ensuring that our graduates are industry-ready from day one. We also place great emphasis on personality development, sports, cultural activities, and community service.

I invite you to explore the many opportunities that our 40-acre campus has to offer. Whether you aspire to be an engineer, a designer, or a technologist, we have a program tailored to help you achieve your dreams.

Together, let us build a brighter future.

Warm regards,
Shri Jagmohan Singh
Principal, Guru Daksh Govt. Polytechnic, Hisar
