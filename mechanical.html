Mechanical Engineering

Diploma in Mechanical Engineering - 3 Year Program

About the Department

The Department of Mechanical Engineering at Guru Daksh Government Polytechnic, Hisar offers a comprehensive 3-year diploma program that covers manufacturing processes, thermodynamics, machine design, automobile engineering, and industrial automation. The department is equipped with modern workshops and laboratories to provide practical training alongside theoretical knowledge, preparing students for careers in manufacturing, automobile, and industrial sectors.

Program Duration

3 Years Diploma (6 Semesters) | Affiliated to HSBTE | AICTE Approved

Key Subjects / Curriculum

  • ✓ Engineering Mechanics & Strength of Materials
  • ✓ Thermodynamics & Fluid Mechanics
  • ✓ Manufacturing Technology & Machine Design
  • ✓ Automobile Engineering
  • ✓ Industrial Automation & CNC Programming
  • ✓ Engineering Drawing & AutoCAD

Important Labs & Facilities

Machine Shop

Equipped with lathe machines, milling machines, drilling machines, and grinding machines for hands-on training.

CNC & CAM Lab

CNC lathe and milling machines with computer-aided manufacturing software for modern machining practice.

Welding Workshop

Arc welding, gas welding, TIG and MIG welding equipment for fabrication training.

Thermal Engineering Lab

IC engines, boiler models, refrigeration and air conditioning units for thermodynamics experiments.

Career Opportunities

  • • Junior Engineer in government departments (PWD, Railways, Defence)
  • • Production / Manufacturing Engineer in industries
  • • Quality Control Inspector in manufacturing units
  • • CNC Programmer / Machine Operator
  • • Automobile Service Engineer
  • • Higher studies (B.Tech Mechanical through lateral entry)
