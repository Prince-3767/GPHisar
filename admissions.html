Admissions 2026-27

Your gateway to a successful career in engineering and technology starts here

How to Apply

Admission Process

1

Online Registration

Register on the HSBTE online portal with your 10th class details and personal information.

2

Document Upload

Upload scanned copies of required documents including marksheets, certificates, and photographs.

3

Counselling

Attend the state-level counselling conducted by DTE Haryana. Choose your preferred branch and institute.

4

Fee Payment & Admission

Pay the semester fee and complete admission formalities at the institute with original documents.

Requirements

Eligibility Criteria

📋 For 1st Year (Direct Entry)

  • ✓ Passed 10th (Matric) with minimum 35% marks
  • ✓ Must have studied Mathematics & Science in 10th
  • ✓ Age: No upper age limit for government polytechnics
  • ✓ Domicile: Haryana residents get preference (HSBTE norms)

📋 For Lateral Entry (2nd Year)

  • ✓ Passed 12th with PCM or ITI (2 year) in relevant trade
  • ✓ Minimum 45% marks (40% for SC/ST/BC candidates)
  • ✓ Direct admission to 2nd year (3rd semester)
Documents

Required Documents

📄 10th Class Marksheet & Certificate (Original + 2 copies)
📄 Character Certificate from School
📄 Haryana Domicile / Residence Certificate
📄 Category Certificate (SC/BC/OBC if applicable)
📄 Income Certificate (for fee concession)
📄 Aadhaar Card (Self and Parents)
📄 Family ID (PPP / Parivar Pehchaan Patra)
📄 Recent Passport Size Photographs (6 copies)
📄 Migration Certificate (if from other board)
📄 Medical Fitness Certificate
