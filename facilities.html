Our Facilities

World-class infrastructure for a complete learning experience

🔬

Laboratories & Workshops

Our institute houses 40+ well-equipped laboratories and workshops across all departments. From computer labs with 300+ systems to mechanical workshops with CNC machines, every facility is designed to provide hands-on learning experience.

  • ✓ Computer Labs with latest hardware & software
  • ✓ Electronics & Communication Lab
  • ✓ Mechanical Workshop with CNC, Lathe, Welding
  • ✓ Civil Engineering Survey & Material Testing Lab
  • ✓ Electrical Machines & Power Systems Lab
🏨

Hostel Accommodation

Separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with a total capacity of 500+ students. Hostels are located within the campus premises, ensuring safety and convenience. Mess facility, hot water, and Wi-Fi are available.

  • ✓ Separate Boys & Girls Hostels
  • ✓ 24/7 Security with CCTV
  • ✓ Mess with nutritious meals
  • ✓ Wi-Fi connectivity
  • ✓ Common room with TV & indoor games
📚

Central Library

A well-stocked central library with over 500 books, journals, and digital resources. The library provides a peaceful environment for study and research, equipped with a digital section for e-learning.

  • ✓ 500+ Books & Reference Material
  • ✓ Digital Library with e-Journals
🖥️

Smart Classrooms

ICT-enabled smart classrooms equipped with projectors, digital boards, and audio-visual systems for interactive learning. These modern facilities enhance the teaching-learning process significantly.

  • ✓ 10+ Smart Classrooms
  • ✓ HD Projectors & Digital Boards
  • ✓ Audio-Visual systems
  • ✓ High-speed internet connectivity
🏟️

Sports Complex

A sprawling multi-sport complex with facilities for cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, badminton, and athletics. Regular inter-college tournaments and sports events are organized to promote fitness and team spirit.

  • ✓ Cricket, Football & Hockey Grounds
  • ✓ Basketball & Volleyball Courts
  • ✓ Indoor Badminton & Table Tennis
  • ✓ Athletic Track & Gymnasium
🌳

Green Campus & Other Facilities

Our 40-acre eco-friendly campus features lush gardens, tree-lined pathways, canteen, medical room, bank ATM, and ample parking. Solar panels and rainwater harvesting make it a sustainable campus.

  • ✓ Canteen with affordable meals
  • ✓ Medical room with first-aid
  • ✓ Bank ATM on campus
  • ✓ Solar-powered & rainwater harvesting
  • ✓ Ample parking for 500+ vehicles
