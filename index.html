About Us

About the Institute

Guru Daksh Government Polytechnic, Hisar was established in 1992 under the Department of Technical Education, Haryana. Spread across a lush 40-acre campus, the institute offers 13+ diploma programs in engineering and technology. With AICTE approval and HSBTE affiliation, it stands as one of the leading polytechnics in North India, producing industry-ready professionals year after year.
